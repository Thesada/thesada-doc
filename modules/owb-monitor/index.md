# OWB Monitor

Monitors a wood-fired hydronic boiler — supply/return temperatures on two loops
and pump current draw. Sends alerts via Telegram and Home Assistant.

**Hardware:** DS18B20 × 4, SCT-013-030 × 2, ADS1115, LILYGO T-SIM7080-S3
